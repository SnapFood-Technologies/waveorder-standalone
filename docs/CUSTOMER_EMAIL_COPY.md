## Email Template Structure

Both email types (Order Placed & Status Updates) include:

1. **Header** - Gradient header with business name
2. **Order Information** - Order number and type
3. **Status/Confirmation Section** - Main message with icon
4. **Order Items** - List of items with quantities and prices
5. **Order Summary** - Total amount
6. **Delivery/Pickup Details** - Address or location information
7. **Postal Pricing Details** (RETAIL only) - Postal service, delivery time, city, country, postal code
8. **Contact Information** - Business phone if available
9. **Footer** - Automated notification disclaimer and copyright

---

## Notes

- All emails are automatically sent based on customer email availability
- Order status emails are configurable per status in admin settings
- Language is determined by the business's language setting (defaults to English if not set)
- RETAIL businesses have special messaging for "Preparing" status ("Preparing Shipment")
- Postal pricing details are only shown for RETAIL businesses when applicable
- Customer emails include full order details, delivery/pickup information, and contact details
