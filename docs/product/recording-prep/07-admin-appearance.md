# Video 07 — Admin: storefront look & feel

**Demo business:** RETAIL (see [README](./README.md)).

**Goal:** Show **Appearance** (or equivalent) settings that change what **customers** see — theme, colors, banner, layout options your product exposes.

**Target length:** ~3–6 min after edit.

---

## On-screen flow (record in order)

| Step | What to show | Notes |
|------|----------------|-------|
| 1 | Admin → **Appearance** | Overview of sections |
| 2 | Change **one or two** visible settings | e.g. color, hero, logo — reversible |
| 3 | Open **storefront** in another tab | Before/after or refresh to show change |

Avoid flipping every toggle; one coherent “make it yours” story.

---

## Script (read aloud or memorize)

**You** = merchant. **~** = pause. Narrate **only** the controls you actually change on screen.

### Appearance (~45 s)

In **admin**, open **Appearance**. **~** This is where **your retail** storefront **looks** like **your brand** — not a generic template. **~** Walk through the **sections** you have: **colors**, **banner**, **logo**, **layout** options — whatever **WaveOrder** exposes for your store.

### Make a visible change (~60 s)

Change **one or two** things that show up clearly on the **public** site — for example **accent color** or **hero** image. **~** **Save** or **apply** per your UI. **~** Switch to the **storefront** tab and **refresh** — **~** your customer now sees the **same** polish you see in admin.

### Close (~15 s)

**Appearance** is how **you** stay **on-brand** on **WaveOrder** — **minutes** in admin, **instant** on the link **you** share.
