# Video 05 — Admin: customers

**Demo business:** RETAIL (see [README](./README.md)).

**Goal:** Show **customer list**, open **one customer**, and **order history** or profile fields the app exposes.

**Target length:** ~3–5 min after edit.

---

## On-screen flow (record in order)

| Step | What to show | Notes |
|------|----------------|-------|
| 1 | **Customers** | List / search optional |
| 2 | Open **one customer** | Contact fields, tags if any |
| 3 | **Order history** or linked orders | Tie back to retail purchases |

---

## Script (read aloud or memorize)

**You** = merchant. **~** = pause.

### Customer list (~40 s)

Open **Customers**. **~** Everyone who ordered through **your retail** storefront shows up here as a **record you own** — not trapped inside a marketplace dashboard. **~** Scroll or **search** to find someone quickly when they message you on **WhatsApp**.

### Profile (~50 s)

Open **one customer**. **~** You see **contact details** and whatever fields your setup collects — **~** enough to recognize **who** they are and **how** to reach them. **~** If you use **tags** or notes, show **one** example so repeat buyers are easy to spot.

### Order history (~40 s)

From the profile, open **order history** or the list of **past orders** linked to this person. **~** You see **what they bought before**, **when**, and **how much** — **~** so you can answer questions, suggest add-ons, or reward **loyalty** without guessing.

### Close (~10 s)

That’s **Customers** on **WaveOrder** — **list**, **profile**, and **history** for **retail**.
