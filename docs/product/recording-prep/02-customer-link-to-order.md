## Script (read aloud or memorize)

Use **second person** toward the **merchant** (“your customer,” “your link”). **~** = short pause. **Bold** = stress. Match on-screen clicks to your actual UI labels.

### Open & browse

Your customer lands on **your storefront** from the link you shared — social, QR code, or text. **~** They see **your branding**, categories, and products laid out for the phone. **~** They scroll, tap a **category** if you use them, and get a clear list — nothing to install, no account required unless your flow asks for it.

### Product & cart

They open a **product**: photos, price, description, and any **options** you set — size, color, add-ons. **~** They tap **add to cart**. **~** From the cart, they can review **line items** and **quantities** before checking out.

### Checkout & order

At checkout, they enter **delivery or pickup** details and **contact info** the way you configured the store — **~** then they **place the order**. **~** They should see a **confirmation** so they know it went through.

### WhatsApp handoff (include only if you record Step 7)

When they choose **Order via WhatsApp**, the app opens **WhatsApp** with a **pre-filled message** — the order summary is already there, so **you** get a structured request instead of a vague “hi, how much?” **~** That’s the handoff from **your catalog** to **the channel you already use**.

### Close (~10 s)

That’s the full path **from your link to a real order** — what your **retail** customers experience on **WaveOrder**.
