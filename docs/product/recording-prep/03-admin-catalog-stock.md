# Video 03 — Admin: catalog & stock

**Demo business:** RETAIL (see [README](./README.md)).

**Goal:** Show how a merchant **adds or edits products**, **categories**, and (if on **Pro**) **inventory / stock** basics.

**Target length:** ~5–8 min after edit.

---

## Script (read aloud or memorize)

Audience: **you** = the merchant. **~** = pause. Say **Save** / **Publish** when your UI actually shows it.

### Products — List (~45 s)

In **admin**, open **Products**, then **List**. **~** This is your **catalog backbone** for a **retail** store: every item your customers can buy. **~** From here you can **find** a product, **edit** it, or add a **new** one — we’ll open one row so you can see how fields map to the storefront.

### Product detail (~90 s)

Inside a product you set the **name**, **price**, **description**, and **images** — what shoppers see on the product page. **~** Assign a **category** so browsing stays organized. **~** If you use **variants** or extra options, configure them here so checkout doesn’t turn into manual back-and-forth. **~** **Save** when you’re done — your live storefront picks this up.

### Categories (~45 s)

Open **Products → Categories**. **~** Categories group your catalog so customers aren’t scrolling one endless list. **~** Create or adjust a category, link products to it — **~** that structure is what makes a **retail** shop feel serious, not improvised.

### Inventory (only if your plan shows **Inventory** — ~60 s)

On **Pro**, open **Inventory** from the sidebar: **dashboard**, **activities**, or **stock adjustment** depending on what you’re demoing. **~** Here you track **what’s on hand** — when something sells, your numbers should reflect reality so you don’t oversell. **~** Walk through **one** adjustment or view so merchants see it’s built in, not a spreadsheet on the side.

### Close (~15 s)

That’s **catalog and stock** for **retail** on **WaveOrder** — products, categories, and inventory when your plan includes it.
