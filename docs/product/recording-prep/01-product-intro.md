## Script (read aloud or memorize)

Use this wording on camera (or adapt a sentence or two to your voice—keep claims aligned with the live site). **~ indicates a short breath.**

### 1 — Who you are + what it is (~35 s)

Hi — I’m **Griseld**, and I’m the **software engineer** behind **WaveOrder**. **~** Here’s a quick introduction to what we built: **WaveOrder** helps **you** sell through a single link — your customers open a proper storefront on their phone, build their order, and send it to the **WhatsApp number you already use**. You **don’t need the WhatsApp Business API** for that — **~** most businesses can go live in **about five minutes**.

### 2 — Problem (~40 s)

If you’ve sold through **Instagram DMs** or **WhatsApp**, you know the friction: **messages pile up**, **details get lost**, and you’re **re-typing the same answers**. **~** If you depend on **delivery apps** or big **marketplaces**, they often take a **large cut** of every sale, **they own the relationship**, and **you don’t keep the customer data** the way you should. **~** You lose **margin** and you lose the **direct line** to people who already want to buy from **you**.

### 3 — What WaveOrder gives merchants (~40 s)

**WaveOrder** gives you a **mobile-friendly catalog and checkout** — so shopping with you feels **professional**, not like a thread of screenshots. **~** You get **notifications** when orders come in, and **insight** into **what sells**, **when people order**, and **who comes back**. **~** You can run **multiple catalogs** for different locations or brands, offer **pickup and delivery time slots**, and **import products with CSV** when you’re setting up or scaling.

### 4 — Who it’s for (~30 s)

We built this for operators who already live on **social** and **chat**: **Instagram sellers**, **restaurants and cafés**, **retail and e-commerce**, **salons and beauty**, and **professional services** that book or take requests over **WhatsApp**. **~** If your customers **find you online** and you **close in chat**, **WaveOrder** matches how you work.

### 5 — Why not delivery apps / marketplaces (~35 s)

Compared to **delivery marketplaces**, **WaveOrder** doesn’t take a **commission** on those orders — **you keep the revenue**. **~** **Your customer data stays yours**, and the **relationship stays direct** on **WhatsApp**. **~** **Setup is minutes**, not **contracts** and long **approval** cycles. **~** We’re not here to be another **middleman** between you and your customer.

### 6 — CTA (~15 s)

If that fits your business, go to **waveorder.app** and start your **fourteen-day free trial**. **Thanks for watching.**
