WaveOrder — short FAQ for chatbot grounding (plain text).
Re-verify pricing at https://waveorder.app/pricing before quoting in production.
Support: support@waveorder.app | Site: https://waveorder.app

Chatbot voice: Say “WaveOrder” or “waveorder.app” to users. Do not say “marketing site” in replies — that phrase is only for internal docs (it means the public site at waveorder.app: home, pricing, sign-up, legal pages). Sign-up and the dashboard are both WaveOrder; the URL for getting started is waveorder.app.

Q: What is WaveOrder?
A: WaveOrder helps businesses sell through a web catalog and take orders on WhatsApp. Customers browse your link, build an order, and open WhatsApp with a pre-filled message to your number. You do not need the WhatsApp Business API for that basic flow.

Q: How do I register or sign up?
A: Go to https://waveorder.app and sign up (register). Then complete the business setup wizard, add your catalog (manually or CSV on typical product plans), connect your WhatsApp number, and share your store link.

Q: How much does WaveOrder cost?
A: Plans in the product are in USD: Starter about $19/mo ($16/mo if billed yearly), Pro about $39/mo ($32/mo yearly), Business about $79/mo ($66/mo yearly). Annual saves about 17%. Always confirm current numbers on waveorder.app/pricing.

Q: Is there a free trial?
A: WaveOrder offers a 14-day trial with access to that plan’s features. Docs say no credit card required to start — confirm in the live signup flow if the user needs certainty.

Q: Do I need the WhatsApp Business API?
A: No for the standard flow. Regular WhatsApp or the WhatsApp Business app works. Orders go to your existing number without API setup or per-message fees for that core path.

Q: How do customers pay?
A: In the product today, merchants configure cash for customer orders (e.g. on delivery, pickup, or when a service is completed — wording depends on business type). Card/PayPal/bank for buyers are not enabled in the merchant setup UI yet; those appear as coming soon. Merchants pay their WaveOrder subscription separately via Stripe (cards, etc.) — that is not the same as customer checkout.

Q: Does WaveOrder take a cut of my sales?
A: Positioning is no commission on your orders; you pay the subscription. For legal certainty, point users to Terms or support.

Q: What business types does WaveOrder support?
A: Restaurant, cafe, retail, grocery, salon, professional services (appointments and requests), and other. Salon/services flows use booking/session language; product businesses use products and CSV import where applicable.

Q: Can I use WaveOrder with Instagram / link in bio?
A: Yes. Put your catalog link in bio, stories, or posts; customers order through WhatsApp.

Q: Can I have more than one store?
A: Starter includes one catalog/store. Pro includes up to five. Business includes unlimited. Each store can have its own link, branding, and WhatsApp number.

Q: What is on the Business plan that Pro does not have?
A: Unlimited stores, team access (about five users in code), custom domain, API access, dedicated support wording. Pro has no custom domain and no API access in plan limits.

Q: How fast can I go live?
A: WaveOrder’s materials say many businesses are live in about five minutes after signup — treat as typical, not a guarantee.

Q: Is there a demo?
A: Yes — try the demo on waveorder.app (e.g. /demo) before signing up.

Q: Where is API documentation?
A: Point developers to https://waveorder.app/developers and API docs (e.g. /api-docs). Do not invent endpoints.

Q: Who do I contact for help?
A: support@waveorder.app. For legal, privacy, or subscription refunds, use Terms and Privacy on waveorder.app or support — do not invent policies.

Q: Can the chatbot access my WaveOrder account?
A: No. For dashboard or billing issues, sign in to WaveOrder or email support.
